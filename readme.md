This repository documents the Universal Carbon Intelligence Platform: a product concept for aggregating registry data, extracting document insights, detecting integrity risks, and surfacing market intelligence for carbon projects.

## Product Summary

The platform sits above existing carbon registries instead of replacing them. It combines:

- Registry ingestion for project, issuance, retirement, and transfer data
- Document extraction from PDDs, monitoring reports, and verification files
- Geospatial analysis for overlaps, land conflicts, and satellite-derived signals
- Integrity detection for dormant projects, anomalies, and double-counting risk
- Risk scoring, due diligence, and portfolio monitoring
- Market intelligence for pricing, liquidity, and buyer signals

## Core User Outcomes

- Buyers and investors can screen projects faster and with more evidence.
- Project developers can see missing data, audit risks, and market visibility.
- Operators can monitor ingestion health, source quality, and model output.
- Analysts can compare projects using consistent integrity and delivery metrics.

## Proposed Platform Modules

1. Registry ingestion dashboard
2. Document extraction and AI insights
3. Geospatial map and overlap analysis
4. Integrity detection center
5. Dynamic risk scoring
6. Market and liquidity signals
7. Automated due diligence summaries
8. Predictive failure and MRV companion
9. Portfolio monitoring
10. Access control, user profile, and administration

## Suggested Delivery Focus

### MVP

### Later Phases
- Liquidity analytics
- Registry account linking
- Automated due diligence exports

- Pricing engine and biodiversity overlays
